About Us

Who We Are

Welcome to the Green University Virtual Gaming Society (GUVGS), a dynamic and vibrant student-driven organization. GUVGS unites passionate gamers and tech enthusiasts at Green University of Bangladesh. Our mission is to build a strong community where creativity, innovation, and leadership thrive through gaming and virtual experiences.

We aim to empower students, provide opportunities for growth, and organize impactful gaming tournaments, workshops, and events. GUVGS stands as a platform for students to excel beyond academics and explore the ever-evolving world of gaming and e-sports.

Our Vision

The vision of the Green University Virtual Gaming Society is to enhance leadership, foster a thriving community of gamers, and develop the professional and technical skills of students at Green University.
